# 🚦 Wrong Turn Violation Dataset

## 📌 Overview
The **Wrong Turn Violation Dataset** is a real-world annotated image dataset designed to support research in **traffic surveillance**, **autonomous vehicles**, and **intelligent transportation systems (ITS)**. The dataset focuses on detecting **wrong-turn traffic violations at road roundabouts**, a critical safety issue that can lead to traffic congestion and accidents.

## 🎯 Key Features
- ✅ Real-world images collected from multiple roundabouts
- ✅ Focus on **vehicle wrong-turn violations**
- ✅ Supports **autonomous driving** and **ITS** research
- ✅ Enables development of **traffic safety applications**
